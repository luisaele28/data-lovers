    Books


    Each chapter a new adventure.

    Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone

    Harry Potter has been orphaned and lives in the home of his abominable uncles and the unbearable cousin Dudley. Harry feels very sad and lonely, until one fine day he receives a letter that will change his life forever. In it he is informed that he has been accepted as a student at Hogwarts School of Magic.

    Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets

    Unaware that someone had opened the Chamber of Secrets, letting out a series of dangerous monsters, Harry and his friends Ron and Hermione will have to face giant spiders, enchanted snakes, enraged ghosts and, above all, the very reincarnation of their most fearsome adversary.

    Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban

    The plot of the book describes that a dangerous assassin, Sirius Black, escaped from Azkaban, the prison of wizards, and is apparently willing to find and kill Harry. It is also known that he is the first person to escape from the wizard prison and no one knows how he did it.

    Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire

    Harry Potter is facing tough times. Beset by nightmares that make his scar hurt more than usual, Harry is happy to escape his restless dreams by being able to attend the Quidditch World Championships and be with his friends Ron and Hermione.

    Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix

    The plot of The Order of the Phoenix revolves around the saga of intrigues that awakens the Ministry of Magic by adopting an official position, which denies the return of Lord Voldemort and, therefore, refuses to take actions that prevent danger.

    Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince

    Set in the sixth year of Harry Potter at Hogwarts College, the play explores the past of the dark wizard Lord Voldemort, as well as the protagonist's preparations along with his mentor Albus Dumbledore for the final battle detailed in the next book, the last in the series.

    Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows

    This last novel narrates the events that directly follow Dumbledore's death, in which Voldemort ends his rise to power and manages to dominate the Ministry of Magic. Harry and his friends decide not to attend their final year at Hogwarts, to go out in search of the remaining horcruxes.
